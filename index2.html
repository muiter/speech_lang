Our Mission

If therapy is not motivating, it will not be successful. We believe in making speech therapy fun and are not afraid to look silly in front of you or your children! Many of the children we serve find communication a daunting and consuming task. At Childhood Speech & Language, our goal is to make communication a valuable tool for your child to explore their world. This is what drives our work here.

To achieve this, we utilize a variety of therapeutic techniques and tools including PROMPT, PECS & Sign Language. We believe in play-based therapy as this is how children learn about the world around them. Together, we can help your child reach their full potential.

We assist children with

  • Apraxia of Speech / Motor Speech Disorders
  • Articulation Disorders
  • Asperger's Syndrome
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
  • Developmental Language Delays
  • Phonological Disorders
  • Receptive and/or Expressive Language Disorders
  • Social Use of Language
